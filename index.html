Autumn at Carlton Gardens

I’m offering a one-day series of Autumn mini sessions at Carlton Gardens in Melbourne on Sunday the 24th of May. Each session is 45 minutes, focused on warm tones, fallen leaves, and relaxed portraits.

For this weekend, I’ll be hiring additional professional camera gear for a wedding on Saturday, and since the equipment can’t be returned until Monday, Sunday sessions will have access to the same high-end setup and lighting.

Shot on dual Sony Alpha 7 R5 bodies with the 24–70mm f/2.8 GM II, 70–200mm f/2.8 GM OSS II, and Profoto A10 flash for clean, professional results in any light.
